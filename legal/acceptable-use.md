# HUDForge Acceptable Use Policy

**Effective Date:** May 26, 2026

**Last Updated:** May 26, 2026

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") is part of the HUDForge [Terms of Service](/legal/terms).

## 1. Purpose

HUDForge helps creators generate Roblox-oriented UI workflows and PNG assets. This policy defines prohibited use of the Service.

## 2. You Must Not

### Abuse generation APIs or credits

- bypass rate limits, credit checks, or plan entitlements;
- use bots or automation to scrape or bulk-extract the Service beyond documented workflows;
- share accounts or resell access in ways that circumvent plan limits;
- attempt to reverse engineer provider integrations to obtain free generation.

### Break the law or violate rights

- use the Service to violate applicable law;
- infringe copyright, trademark, privacy, or other third-party rights;
- submit content you do not have permission to use.

### Harm people or communities

- harass, threaten, or defame others;
- generate content intended to exploit or sexualize minors;
- create deceptive impersonation or fraud materials.

### Compromise security

- introduce malware, phishing kits, or credential theft tools;
- attempt unauthorized access to accounts, systems, or infrastructure;
- probe, overload, or disrupt HUDForge services.

### Misuse AI output

- use output without reviewing it for platform compliance and safety;
- intentionally generate content designed to infringe protected brands or proprietary game assets.

## 3. Roblox-Specific Expectations

You are responsible for ensuring prompts, assets, and exports comply with Roblox rules. HUDForge does not approve or certify Roblox compliance.

## 4. Enforcement

We may investigate violations and take action including:

- blocking generations or exports;
- suspending or terminating accounts;
- cancelling subscriptions without refund where permitted; and
- reporting unlawful conduct to providers or authorities where appropriate.

## 5. Reporting Abuse

Report abuse to **support@hudforge.app** with relevant account details and evidence.
