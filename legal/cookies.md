# HUDForge Cookie Policy

**Effective Date:** May 26, 2026

**Last Updated:** May 26, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how HUDForge uses cookies and similar technologies on hudforge.app and related authenticated pages.

## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. Similar technologies include local storage, session storage, and analytics pixels.

## 2. Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

- keep you signed in and maintain secure sessions;
- remember preferences and consent choices;
- protect the Service from abuse; and
- measure site and product usage where enabled.

## 3. Cookies We Use

### Strictly necessary

Required for core functionality, including **Clerk** authentication cookies that maintain your signed-in session and protect account access.

### Analytics (optional)

If enabled, we may use **Google Analytics 4 (GA4)** to understand traffic sources, page performance, and product conversion. These cookies are not required to use the Service.

We do not use marketing retargeting cookies unless we explicitly enable them and disclose that here.

## 4. Third-Party Technologies

Depending on how you use HUDForge, cookies or similar storage may be set by:

- **Clerk** — authentication and session handling;
- **Google Analytics 4** — optional usage analytics; and
- infrastructure or security tools required to operate the site.

Third-party providers may process data according to their own policies.

## 5. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling strictly necessary cookies may prevent sign-in or account access.

Where required by law, we will ask for consent before setting non-essential analytics cookies.

## 6. Changes

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Check the "Last Updated" date above.
