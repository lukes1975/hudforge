# HUDForge Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** May 26, 2026

**Last Updated:** May 26, 2026

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how **HUDForge** ("HUDForge," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and shares personal data when you use our website, account workspace, generation workflows, billing, and support channels (collectively, the "Service").

If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the Service.

## 2. Personal Data We Collect

Depending on how you use HUDForge, we may collect:

### Account and identity data

- email address and account identifiers;
- authentication data from our identity provider;
- account preferences and settings.

### Prompt, content, and generation data

- prompts, UI instructions, and uploaded references;
- generation history, previews, exports, and related metadata;
- support messages and feedback.

### Transaction and billing data

- subscription status and plan;
- order and customer identifiers from our payment processor;
- billing status, invoices, refunds, and tax-related metadata.

We do **not** intentionally store full payment card numbers. Card data is handled by Lemon Squeezy and its payment partners.

### Technical and usage data

- IP address, browser, device, and operating system;
- page views, feature usage, session events, and referral data;
- error logs, security telemetry, and performance data.

## 3. How We Collect Data

We collect data:

- directly from you when you sign up, use the Service, or contact support;
- automatically through cookies, logs, and similar technologies; and
- from service providers that help us operate the Service.

## 4. How We Use Personal Data

We use personal data to:

- provide and maintain the Service;
- authenticate users and secure accounts;
- process prompts, generations, previews, and exports;
- manage subscriptions, credits, billing, and refunds;
- send transactional emails and support replies;
- improve reliability, performance, and product quality;
- detect abuse, fraud, and security incidents; and
- comply with legal obligations.

## 5. Service Providers We Use

HUDForge relies on trusted processors to operate the Service, including:

- **Clerk** — authentication and session management;
- **Supabase** — database storage for accounts, generations, credits, and billing records;
- **Lemon Squeezy** — subscription billing, top-up purchases, and payment processing;
- **OpenRouter** — prompt optimization (DeepSeek and related models);
- **FAL.ai** — PNG game UI asset generation;
- **Sentry** — error monitoring and application diagnostics;
- **Resend** — transactional email delivery;
- hosting and infrastructure providers supporting hudforge.app.

We share only the data needed for each provider to perform its function.

## 6. AI Processing Notice

When you run generation workflows, your prompts and related inputs may be transmitted to AI providers (such as OpenRouter and FAL.ai) to produce optimized specs and PNG assets.

Do not submit sensitive personal data, regulated data, or third-party content you are not authorized to share unless we explicitly support that use case in writing.

## 7. Cookies and Analytics

We use cookies and similar technologies for authentication, security, and site functionality. We may use optional analytics (such as Google Analytics 4) to understand product usage and improve conversion.

See our [Cookie Policy](/legal/cookies) for details.

## 8. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as reasonably necessary to:

- provide the Service;
- maintain billing and tax records;
- resolve disputes and enforce agreements;
- investigate abuse; and
- meet legal obligations.

When you delete your account, we delete or anonymize personal data where feasible, except data we must retain for legal, security, or backup integrity reasons.

## 9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or port your personal data, or to object to certain processing.

To exercise these rights, email privacy@hudforge.app. We may need to verify your identity before responding.

## 10. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards for cross-border transfers.

## 11. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal data, including access controls, encrypted connections (HTTPS), and provider-managed security features. No system is completely secure.

## 12. Children's Privacy

HUDForge is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided data unlawfully, contact privacy@hudforge.app.

## 13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the "Last Updated" date and provide additional notice for material changes where appropriate.
